## 🎯 Quick Navigation

### Public Pages (No Login Required)
- **Landing Page**: `/` - Homepage with value proposition
- **Explore Assets**: `/explore` - Discover featured investments
- **Marketplace**: `/marketplace/listings` - Browse all assets
- **Login**: `/auth/login` - User authentication
- **Sign Up**: `/auth/signup` - New account creation

### User Pages (Login Required)
- **Dashboard**: `/dashboard` - Portfolio overview
- **Portfolio**: `/portfolio` - Detailed holdings & analytics
- **Marketplace**: `/marketplace/listings` - Investment opportunities
- **Secondary Market**: `/marketplace/secondary` - Trade with other users
- **Transactions**: `/transactions` - Complete transaction history
- **Settings**: `/settings` - Profile & account management

### Admin Pages (Admin Role)
- **Admin Dashboard**: `/admin/dashboard` - Platform overview
- **Verification Queue**: `/admin/verification` - Review KYC & listings
- **Manage Listings**: `/admin/listings` - Approve/reject assets
- **User Management**: `/admin/users` - Monitor investors

   - Selfie verification

   - Proof of address
   - Income verification
   - Investment experience
